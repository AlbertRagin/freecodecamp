Leninigorsk

the best city in tatarstan

Here's a time line of Leninigors city:

  • 1917-foundation
  • 1955-first oil plants & factories
  • 1960s-a sewing factory, an aggregate-mechanical plant, a Radiopribor plant, the first and second oil fields of NGDU Leninogorskneft were created. During these years, capital housing construction has developed rapidly. the city is mainly built up with four-storey residential buildings.
  • 1970s-a new stage in urban planning, a new master plan was adopted, timed to coincide with the twenty-fifth anniversary of the city. There was a complete reconstruction of Leningradskaya, Tukaya, Sverdlov streets, Lenin Avenue.
  • novadays-winner of the All-Russian competition "The most comfortable city in Russia" among cities with a population of less than 100 thousand people: in 2004 and 2007. The city, called the green pearl of Tatarstan in terms of the number of green spaces per person, occupies one of the first places in the country, their total area within the city limits is 14,232 km, which is almost half of the total area of the city.

Incomparable to anything, The pride of the Motherland – It is rich in gas and oil. In the daisies of the valley, Ripe fields – A city that looks like a garden.
